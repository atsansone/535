Links

Now, let's learn about what makes a webpage truly a webpage, links. If not for links, a webpage would be a static document isolated from the rest of the internet nearly impossible to reach and just as impossible to leave. Links are what makes the world wide web, the world wide web. Now that we've stressed the importance of learning such a critical component of webpages, let go take a look at three types of links: in page links or bookmarks, local page links, and external links.

Bookmarks

Bookmarks are links found in a webpage that take you to other parts of the same page. Links are formed with the start tag <a href> but Here, we distinguish from links that take you to another page within the same website.
