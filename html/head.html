Head, Title, Meta, Comments

Head

As you can see in the project HTML, the tag comes right after the <html> start tag is the <head> start tag. Look a little below, you can find the <head> end tag. The section enclosed in these tags is called the HEAD of the document and contains important information for the web designer and the browser to know but does not necessarily get displayed for the public. Here, you will include META information (see below) as well as links to stylesheets and scripts (though this could be placed below the body as well).

Title

The title tags (<title> and </title>) let the document know what the title of document is. Depending on you browser, the title is typically displayed at the very top of the browser window, or if you are using browser tabs, in the tab. It helps orient the user.

Meta

Meta information is any extra information about the particular webpage that describes the document or provides helpful explanations for future reference. Meta information is enclosed in the <meta> and </meta> tags. Let's take a look at the meta information for our project.
